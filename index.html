about

I am the creator behind midoumao — drawn to the quiet beauty of everyday life, the warmth of animals, and the slow, deliberate craft of making things worth keeping.

By nature, I am calm and observant. I prefer depth over noise, and meaning over volume. Whether I am writing a piece about a cat's morning ritual, perfecting a hand-crafted pet food recipe, or following a thread of curiosity to its end — I move with intention.

My world is shaped by three things: animals, who teach me patience; words, which give shape to feeling; and the internet, which I still believe can be a place of genuine connection and quiet discovery.

I build slowly, but I build with care. These are the places that carry that care.
